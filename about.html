As a leading furniture maker, we see it as our duty to promote responsible consumption. This is reflected in our

ofounding principles, charitable fund ownership, and commitment to the UN Global Compact's 10 principles.

Fritz Hansen has been ISO 14001 certified since 2004.

ISO 14001 is an internationally agreed standard that sets out the

requirements for an environmental management system.

It helps organizations improve their environmental performance through

more efficient use of resources and reduction of waste, gaining

a competitive advantage and the trust of stakeholders.

By collaborating with designers, partners and suppliers who share

our values, we merge our expertise to drive positive, sustainable change.
